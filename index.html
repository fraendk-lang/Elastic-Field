About Elastic Field

Elastic Field was founded in 2011 – a free-form music project rooted in ambient, lounge, trip hop, downbeat, jazz, and subtle electronic textures.

In 2012: Non Smokers Nightmare. 2013: Another Non Smokers Nightmare. 2025: new remixes & compositions.
